#BlogoText

This is BlogoText, the lightweight SQLite Blog-Engine.
Features

  * Blog with comments and RSS feeds
  * Links sharing
  * RSS Reader
  * Images/Files uploading and sharing
  * JSON/ZIP/HTML import-export; WordPress import
  * Support Addons
